# OpenDoorDesign Standards V1

> Version 1.0

This document is the executive overview of the Open Door Design standards. Detailed guidance is contained in the Standards, Patterns, Components, and Philosophy folders.

## Core Principles

- The software works for the user.
- Accessibility is the foundation.
- Compliance is the minimum. Usability is the goal.
- Consistency builds confidence.
- Design for people before technology.

## Repository Structure

- Philosophy
- Standards
- Patterns
- Components
- Assets
- Templates
- Examples
- Resources
- Archive

## Living Standard

This repository is the single source of truth for Open Door Design. Standards evolve through real-world development. When a reusable solution is created, it is documented here before being adopted by applications.
